Thank you for your interest in my staging services. I pride myself on the ability I have to educate the homeowner on the importance of preparing the home they wish to sell. I am a big believer in the old saying, You can never make a second first impression. Having the input of an experienced and professionally trained stager will help the homeowner offer a home that is ready for listing photos and showings. I have lived in San Antonio for 25 years after growing up in Austin and graduating from Texas AM University, class of 82! I have worked as a pre-school teacher, done custom faux painting and volunteered as PTA President and Spirit Club President at my daughters schools. I started assisting a home stager and kept working on my own after she moved out of state. I have attended two training classes provided by the Creator of Home Staging and am an ASP, Accredited Staging Professional.
